Privacy Policy

Track & Tide is built with privacy in mind. This page explains what data we handle and how we handle it — which is to say, very little.

🔒 No personal data collection

Track & Tide does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no sign-ups, and no forms that ask for personal information.

What we store locally

  • Theme preference (light/dark mode) — saved in your browser's localStorage so the site remembers your choice on return visits.
  • Satellite view preference — also saved locally so your map view choice persists.

All of this data stays on your device. It never leaves your browser and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-party services

The site loads map tiles and data from these providers to function:

  • CARTO — provides the default map style (Positron / Dark Matter)
  • Esri — provides satellite imagery when you enable satellite view
  • OpenStreetMap / OpenRailwayMap — rail infrastructure data
  • GitHub (raw.githubusercontent.com) — country boundary GeoJSON
  • Open-Meteo — weather data
  • Firebase (Firestore) — stores user-submitted comments on stations and ports

These services may see your IP address as part of standard web requests, which is necessary for the map to function. None of these services are used for tracking or advertising purposes.

Geolocation

If you tap the "Locate me" button, your browser will ask for permission to access your location. This is handled entirely by your browser — the coordinates are used only to centre the map and are never stored or transmitted anywhere.

Cookies

Track & Tide does not set any cookies. Not for tracking, not for analytics, not for anything. Your preferences are stored in localStorage, which is not the same as cookies and cannot be read by third parties.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out via the report form on any page.
